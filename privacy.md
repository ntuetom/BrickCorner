# Privacy Policy

BrickCorner does not require users to create an account or provide personal information.

---

## Information Collection and Use

The app may collect anonymous usage data, such as app interactions and crash reports, to improve stability and user experience. This data does not identify individual users.

---

## Advertising

BrickCorner may display advertisements provided by third-party ad networks. These services may use device identifiers in accordance with Apple’s App Tracking Transparency framework.

Users can choose whether to allow tracking when prompted by the system.

---

## Purchases

Premium subscriptions and in-app purchases are processed by Apple and linked to the user’s Apple ID. BrickCorner does not collect or store payment information.

---

## Restoring Purchases

If users switch devices, Premium status can be restored by signing in with the same Apple ID.
